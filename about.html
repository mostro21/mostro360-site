The Focus

Why Mortgage Brokers

The mortgage industry sits at the intersection of three explosive liability factors — and almost no one is protecting against all three simultaneously.

✓
The MLO Problem

1099 loan officers on personal devices, handling NPI, with no oversight, no audit trail, and no documentation. Every departure is a liability event waiting to happen.

✓
The Regulatory Gap

FTC Safeguards Rule. Florida FIPA. Florida OFR exam authority. Three regulatory frameworks hitting simultaneously — and most brokers are exposed on all three.

✓
The AI Blind Spot

Your LOs are already using ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini with borrower data. You have no visibility, no control, and no audit trail. That's not a future problem — it's happening right now.
