The Problem

Your Brokerage Is One of the Most Targeted Businesses in America. Most Are Protected Like a Small Retail Shop.

Mortgage brokerages handle some of the most valuable data that exists. Social Security numbers. Tax returns. Bank statements. Credit histories. Employment records. Property information. All of it sitting across email inboxes, cloud storage, loan origination systems, and the personal devices of a distributed remote workforce.

To a cybercriminal — your brokerage is an extraordinarily attractive target. The data you handle is worth significantly more on the black market than a typical small business.

The wire transfers involved in real estate transactions make your clients targets for fraud. And the regulatory environment you operate in means a breach does not just cost you money — it can cost you your license, your reputation, and your ability to operate.

Most Brokerages Respond With:

Basic antivirus software. A password policy nobody follows. And the hope that nothing happens. That is not a cybersecurity strategy. That is a liability waiting to materialize.
