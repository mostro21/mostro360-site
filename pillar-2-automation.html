The Phone Number

One Number. Every Device. Total Flexibility.

Every LO receives a dedicated business phone number through the platform — separate from their personal cell and fully theirs to build their business on.

This number works across every device they use. They can make and receive calls directly from their computer through the platform. Through the MOSTRO 360 mobile app on their phone. And when away from their desk — calls forward directly to their personal cell.

This number is not a company asset. It is the LO's professional identity on your platform. Their clients know it. Their realtors call it. Their pipeline is built around it.

And if they ever leave your brokerage — they take it with them. Their contacts, their call history, and their professional relationships go with them — exported cleanly, documented properly, transferred to wherever they land next.

For the LO

For the Broker

Every LO departure is documented. Every contact handoff is clean. Every file is accounted for. The audit trail stays with your brokerage. The disputes that typically follow LO departures are dramatically reduced — because the process was designed to be clean from day one.

LOS Integration

Designed to Work With the System You're Already Running.

MOSTRO 360 is designed to connect with ARIVE — the loan origination system built specifically for independent mortgage brokers. When a loan milestone occurs in ARIVE the platform is designed to trigger the corresponding automated communication in GHL — without manual input from your LO.
